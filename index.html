About me

I listen to understand, never to judge.

People often find themselves opening up to me — their struggles, their fears, the things they've never said aloud. I believe everyone deserves to be met with kindness, even those the world finds hard to love.

My mind is wired to wonder why — why we behave the way we do, what shapes a child's inner world, and how the right words at the right moment can help someone feel understood.

With a background in Mathematics and Statistics and over 8 years coordinating at an international school, I bring both analytical clarity and deep warmth to my work with people, especially children and the vulnerable.

When I'm not listening, you'll find me singing, dancing, and laughing with children — or volunteering on 7 Cups, simply being there for someone who needs a kind ear.

Gentle tools AI assisted

Little companions for reflection

These tools are here to help you reflect and find words — not to replace real human support. For anything heavy on your heart, I'd love for you to reach out to me directly.

Reflective Journaling Prompt

Tell me a little about how you're feeling today, and I'll offer a gentle prompt to explore in your journal.

A reflection tool only — not therapy or advice. If you're struggling, please reach out to Sathyapriya or a professional.

Help Me Say It Kindly

Describe something you need to say — to a child, a partner, anyone — and I'll suggest a gentle, compassionate way to word it.

Suggestions to inspire your own words — you know your people best.

Find What You Need

Not sure where to begin? Share what's bringing you here and I'll gently point you toward the right next step.

A friendly signpost, not a diagnosis. Booking a real conversation is always the best next step.
